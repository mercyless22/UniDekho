About MIT

MIT, established in 1861, is known worldwide for its academic and research excellence. It offers a wide range of programs across science, engineering, arts, and business. With state-of-the-art facilities, MIT has been a leader in technological innovations. It is situated in Cambridge, Massachusetts, a hub for education and research.

Admission Process

The admission process at MIT is highly competitive, focusing on academic excellence, leadership, and extracurricular activities. Students must submit standardized test scores (SAT/ACT), transcripts, and recommendation letters. International students are also required to provide proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS).
